## How to pick the next one

Before writing, quickly check search demand with a free tool (Google Keyword
Planner or Ubersuggest's free tier) for phrases like:
- "how to build a [thing] in [language]"
- "[thing] tutorial [language]"

Prioritize topics with real monthly searches and few strong existing guides
(check the first page of Google — if it's dominated by big sites like
freeCodeCamp or major tutorial hubs, it'll be harder to rank; thinner
competition is a green light).

## Per-post checklist

- [ ] Target keyword in the title, naturally
- [ ] Working code, tested before publishing
- [ ] A "where to take it next" section (keeps readers engaged, sets up
      future posts, and is genuinely useful)
- [ ] 2-4 relevant tags
- [ ] Internal link to at least one other post once you have a few published
